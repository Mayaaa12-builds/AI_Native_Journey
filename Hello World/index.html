The Astoria Parking Problem

We know the drill: circling blocks, endless searches, and the frustration of finding a spot in Astoria. It's not just an inconvenience; it impacts your daily life, causes delays, and contributes to street congestion.

For tenants, homeowners without private garages, and visitors alike, finding parking is a constant challenge. Limited spaces, 'no parking' zones, fire hydrants, and private entrances further shrink the available spots.

Your App's Superpowers

Smart Neighbor Matching

Our app intelligently connects you with neighbors within 3 blocks who share similar driving habits or destinations, making carpooling a breeze.

Personalized Usage Surveys

Quickly tell us your car usage days, times, and common destinations to get the most relevant matches for your needs.

Secure & Verified Connections

Each user is verified for safety, ensuring you connect with trusted neighbors for a secure carpooling experience.

Real-time Parking Insights for Visitors

Visitors can check in for expected arrival times and receive live feedback from residents on street parking availability.

Parking Hotspot Predictions

Report peak parking struggle times, and the app will provide predictions, helping everyone avoid the busiest hours.

Continuous Improvement via Feedback

Easily provide feedback after each trip, helping us continuously refine the app and adapt to your needs.

Build a Stronger Astoria Community

Connect with Neighbors

Beyond just carpooling, our app encourages residents to connect, share resources, and help each other out, fostering a true sense of community right where you live.

Save Time & Reduce Stress

Spend less time circling for parking and more time doing what you love. By coordinating rides, you can significantly reduce the daily parking struggle for everyone.
